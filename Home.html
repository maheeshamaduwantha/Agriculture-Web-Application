Agricultural Deparment

Department of Agriculture in Sri Lanka plays a pivotal role in the country's agricultural sector. Responsible for the development and implementation of agricultural policies, the department focuses on enhancing productivity, promoting sustainable practices, and ensuring food security. Services provided include crop monitoring, soil testing, water management guidance, livestock support, research and development initiatives, financial assistance programs, market access facilitation, and education services.

The department also emphasizes environmental stewardship, encouraging practices that balance agricultural productivity with ecological sustainability.
